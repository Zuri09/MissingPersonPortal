Using Shodan to Aid in Finding Missing Persons

Shodan is a search engine for internet-connected devices. While it may not be directly related to finding missing persons, it can help identify online footprints or security vulnerabilities.

  1. Visit the Shodan website: Go to the Shodan website at https://www.shodan.io.
  2. Enter specific search terms: Input search terms related to the missing person or their online presence into the search bar.
  3. Examine the search results: Review the search results to identify internet-connected devices or online footprints that may be relevant to the search.
