Using Spokeo to Find Missing Persons

Spokeo aggregates data from various sources, including social media, to provide information about individuals.

  1. Go to the Spokeo website: Visit the Spokeo website at https://www.spokeo.com.
  2. Enter the known details about the missing person: Input details such as their name, email address, or phone number in the provided search fields.
  3. Click the "Search" button: Click the "Search" button to generate a list of possible matches.
  4. Examine the results: Review the results to find contact information or social media profiles associated with the individual.
