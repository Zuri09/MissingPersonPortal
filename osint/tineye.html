TinEye: Reverse Image Search

TinEye is a reverse image search engine that can help you locate other instances of a photo or image of the missing person on the internet.

  1. Visit the TinEye website: Go to the TinEye website at https://tineye.com.
  2. Upload an Image or Provide an Image URL: You can perform a reverse image search by either:
    • Uploading an image directly from your computer by clicking the "Upload" button and selecting the image file.
    • Entering the URL of an image hosted online by clicking the "Enter image URL" tab and pasting the image's web address.
  3. Click "Search": After uploading the image or providing the URL, click the "Search" button to initiate the reverse image search.
  4. Review Search Results: TinEye will display a list of websites and pages where the image or similar images have been found. This can help you identify where the image has been used or find similar images.
  5. Analyze the Results: Examine the search results to gather information about the image's origin, potential copyright violations, or the context in which it has been used.
